Notes to Appendix VI - Lesson 16

In Lesson 16, Mickey is showing us how to vary the Symmetric Introductions and Endings with various tricks.  He is using Exercises 33, 34 and 32 (the order that they appear in this lesson) as examples and varies each in different ways.  The lesson is pretty straight forward.  The toughest part is just playing the exercises and committing them to memory.

Additional Exercises:  Mickey tasks us with using these examples in arrangements we may know. 

Hope you're having as much fun with this as you can stand!!
